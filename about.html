"Namaste and Welcome to Ray Yoga! The way of living towards a healthy mind in a healthy body!

"Poonam began her yoga journey In 2002, while studying her bachelors degree in ayurveda. Since then she has tried different styles of yoga and also has been certified in a few of the yoga styles.

She has been teaching yoga to adults as well as kids since last 5 years. To keep the kids interested, she has also choreographed yoga performances for the kids. Her students have been performing yoga on stage at different events.

Other than passion for yoga , Poonam has other passions too. She has been choreographing and also performing on bollywood dances. She also enjoys playing table tennis. Along with this she is training in martial arts with her Son.

She likes to stay active and loves learning and challenging herself. She believes yoga has the power to bring peace and love in this world..
